return policy

No cancellations will be entertained after the product has been dispatched. Products are returnable within the return window of 7 days of delivery only in such cases where you have received a product different from what you have ordered. We will get the product replaced in such cases. However, we will not issue refund for such orders. To get the product replaced, you have to dispatch the product to our address and a replacement request has to be made at oshodarshandelhi@gmail.com with your email ID, order details and phone number or by calling us on +91 9013697841.
